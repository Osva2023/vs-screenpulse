ScreenPulse

Self-updating product screenshots for docs, sales pages, and app stores.

#saas #devtools #productmarketing #automation #docs

The Problem

Product teams waste time manually retaking screenshots whenever UI changes, causing stale docs, broken marketing pages, and inaccurate app listings.

Our Solution

ScreenPulse connects to staging or production apps, watches target flows, regenerates polished screenshots automatically after UI changes, and syncs them into docs and marketing assets.

Key Features

  • Flow-based screenshot capture
  • Automatic change detection from deploys or commits
  • Branded device frames and annotations
  • One-click sync to docs, CMS, and app store asset folders
  • Approval workflow before publishing updated screenshots
